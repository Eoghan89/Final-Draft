For the love of music

Music Producer and Ableton Music Wiz - Action GT, will be kicking off some post-lockdown dates starting in the capital's 3 Arena playing a succession of nights before moving on and taking the tour around some of Ireland's best known Music venues. A lover of classic electro music with an emphasis on 80's synth and a keen innovator of all things digital, ActionGT has a litany of successful gigs under his belt - bound to have the audience pumping!

The electronic producer and synth star will be giving live demo's and break out session in some of the smaller venues with the Create Sound team allowing a select few in on the secrets to using the latest hardware and software in digital music technology. GT has an intricate knowledge of Ableton Live while incorporating the full Abelton suite.

Mixes are vibrant and varied with multi tools such as Push, APC40 and other well known midi controllers. Before GT smashes the venues he will be giving intros into Basic Track build for live recording and instrumentals as well as how to become as successful on the Abelton controls as he has!

Break out sessions will run for a select few over a two hour period and first 100 tickets will secure your place.
